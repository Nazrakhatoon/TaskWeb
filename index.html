Our Story

Founded in 2025, Saffron Bistro brings the vibrant flavors of India to the heart of the city. Our chef, with over 20 years of experience, combines traditional recipes with modern techniques to create unforgettable dining experiences.

We source our ingredients locally whenever possible, ensuring freshness and supporting our community.
